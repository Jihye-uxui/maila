Meet Maila

Hi guys, I'm Maila, the owner of Maila Cake. We are known for it’s clean, modern yet elegant vibe and finely curated high-quality desserts. The Maila revels in the belief that fresh is best, creating fine pastries daily to the community.

The daily menu is composed of a variety of macarons, pastries, cakes, mousse cakes, tarts, cream puffs, signature Just Jars, and more. Raveena & the team at Maila Cake strives to bring excellence and perfection to each dessert and cake that is created in the Maila Vegan Cake.

The Maila team consists of world-class pastry chefs, Red Seal certified, bringing the highest level of pastry knowledge to the kitchen. And now, the Maila team carries a national title of THE BEST VEGAN CAKES from Food Network Canada’s series “The Maila”.

Our main goal is to create a culture of appreciation around the art of pastry, all while using the best ingredients to conjure the most delicious of desserts.
